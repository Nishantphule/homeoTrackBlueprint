10. Long Term Vision

5-year vision for HomeoTrack and the homeopathy ecosystem

Our Vision

To become the digital infrastructure for homeopathy care in India, enabling every homeopathy doctor and patient to deliver and receive better treatment outcomes through technology.

Years 1-2: Foundation & Maharashtra

  • Establish strong presence in Maharashtra with 500+ doctors and 50K+ patients
  • Build core platform features: AI prescription scanner, bottle recognition
  • Achieve ₹2-3 Cr ARR and break-even
  • Build brand recognition in homeopathy community

Years 3-4: Pan-India Expansion

  • Expand to top 10 Indian states (Delhi, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, etc.)
  • Reach 5,000+ doctors and 5L+ patients
  • Launch teleconsultation and pharmacy marketplace
  • Achieve ₹25-30 Cr ARR

Year 5+: Platform & Ecosystem

  • Become the default platform for homeopathy clinics (20K+ doctors)
  • Build complete ecosystem: EMR, billing, inventory, telemedicine
  • Launch API platform for third-party integrations
  • Expand internationally (Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Middle East)
  • Achieve ₹100 Cr+ ARR and become profitable
