6. Business Model

B2B SaaS, D2C freemium, and marketplace revenue models with pricing tiers

Revenue Stream 1: B2B SaaS (Doctors)

Primary revenue source

B2B

Basic Plan

₹999/month

  • • Up to 50 patients
  • • Basic prescription management
  • • Email support

Professional

₹2,499/month

  • • Unlimited patients
  • • Advanced analytics
  • • Priority support
  • • API access

Clinic Plan

₹4,999/month

  • • Multiple doctors
  • • Clinic management
  • • Custom branding
  • • Dedicated support

Target: 100 doctors in Year 1 at ₹2,499/month average = ₹30L ARR

Revenue Stream 2: D2C Freemium (Patients)

Secondary revenue source

D2C

Free Tier

  • • Basic reminders
  • • Single prescription
  • • Limited medicine library
  • • Ads supported

Premium - ₹199/month

  • • Unlimited prescriptions
  • • Family management
  • • Advanced reminders
  • • No ads
  • • Medicine bottle scanner

Target: 5% conversion rate from 10K free users = 500 premium users × ₹199 = ₹10L ARR

Revenue Stream 3: Marketplace Commission

Future revenue source

Marketplace

Commission Model

  • 10-15% commission on medicine orders through platform
  • Pharmacy partnerships for delivery
  • Teleconsultation booking fees (20% commission)

Target: Year 2+ revenue stream as platform scales

Unit Economics (Year 1)

Revenue

  • B2B SaaS (100 doctors) ₹30L
  • D2C Premium (500 users) ₹10L
  • Total ARR ₹40L

Costs

  • Infrastructure & Hosting ₹2L
  • Team (3-4 people) ₹20L
  • Marketing & Sales ₹5L
  • Total Costs ₹27L

Net Profit: ₹13L (32.5% margin)
