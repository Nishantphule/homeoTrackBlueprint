4. Product Roadmap

5-phase roadmap from MVP to full platform with timelines and feature details

Phase V1 – MVP

Months 1–3

Foundation
Progress 0%

Features

  • Doctor creates prescription with basic medicine details
  • Patient receives medicine reminders via push notifications
  • Basic dose tracking with confirmation button
  • Simple notification system (push + SMS)
  • Basic patient dashboard showing active prescriptions

Goal: Validate core usage and prove that doctors and patients will use the platform.

Phase V2 – Homeopathy Intelligence

Months 3–6

Enhanced
Progress 0%

Features

  • Potency tracking (30C, 200C, 1M) with visual indicators
  • Before/after food reminders with meal timing integration
  • Weekly dose scheduling (e.g., "Every Sunday morning")
  • Medicine image library with brand identification
  • Adherence analytics dashboard for doctors

Goal: Make the platform truly homeopathy-specific and differentiate from generic medicine apps.

Phase V3 – AI Prescription Scanner

Months 6–9

AI-Powered
Progress 0%

Features

  • Upload prescription image (photo or scan)
  • OCR medicine extraction using AI/ML
  • Auto schedule generation based on prescription text
  • Patient digital prescription archive

Goal: Reduce manual entry friction and make onboarding seamless for patients.

Phase V4 – Bottle Recognition

Months 9–12

Computer Vision
Progress 0%

Features

  • Scan medicine bottle using phone camera
  • Identify brand and remedy using computer vision
  • Add to schedule automatically
  • Medicine inventory tracking

Goal: Simplify patient experience and solve the "similar bottles" problem.

Phase V5 – Full Health Platform

Year 2

Complete Platform
Progress 0%

Features

  • Teleconsultation integration with video calls
  • Medicine ordering through partner pharmacies
  • Pharmacy integration and delivery tracking
  • Advanced patient analytics dashboard
  • Clinic management tools (appointments, billing)

Goal: Become the digital infrastructure for homeopathy clinics and create a complete ecosystem.
