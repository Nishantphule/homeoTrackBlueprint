Doctor Dashboard (Web App)

Create Digital Prescriptions

Template-based prescription creation with homeopathy remedy database, potency selection (30C, 200C, 1M), and dosage instructions.

Assign Medicine Schedule

Set custom schedules: daily, weekly, before/after food, specific times. Automatically syncs to patient app.

Potency Tracking

Track and manage different potencies (6C, 30C, 200C, 1M) with visual indicators and potency-specific reminders.

Adherence Tracking

Real-time dashboard showing patient adherence rates, missed doses, and treatment progress.

Patient History

Complete treatment history, previous prescriptions, adherence patterns, and patient notes for better continuity of care.

Patient Mobile App (iOS & Android)

Smart Medicine Reminders

Intelligent push notifications with medicine name, dosage, potency, and food instructions. Customizable reminder times.

Visual Medicine Identification

Photo library of medicine bottles with color-coded labels. Quick visual reference to identify correct medicine.

Dose Confirmation

One-tap confirmation when medicine is taken. Tracks adherence and sends data to doctor dashboard.

Prescription View

Digital copy of prescription with all medicines, schedules, and instructions. Accessible anytime.

Family Medicine Management

Manage prescriptions for multiple family members from one account. Switch between profiles easily.
