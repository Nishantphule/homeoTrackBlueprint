  • • Target: 10,000 app users in Year 1

  • Digital Health Adoption: Post-COVID, 60% of Indians are more open to digital health solutions
  • Smartphone Penetration: 70%+ smartphone users in urban Maharashtra
  • Homeopathy Growth: Increasing preference for natural, side-effect-free treatments
  • Telemedicine: Growing acceptance of digital consultations (30% YoY growth)

Competitive Landscape

Current State: No dedicated homeopathy prescription management platform exists in India

  • Generic medicine reminder apps exist but lack homeopathy-specific features
  • EMR systems are too complex and expensive for small clinics
  • Opportunity: First-mover advantage in homeopathy-specific digital tools
