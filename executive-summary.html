1. Executive Summary

Overview of HomeoTrack concept, target users, core problem, and proposed solution

The Concept

HomeoTrack is a digital platform that helps patients follow complex homeopathy prescriptions using smart reminders, visual medicine identification, and seamless doctor integration. The platform bridges the gap between homeopathy doctors and their patients, ensuring better treatment adherence and outcomes.

Target Users

  • • Homeopathy doctors (primary)
  • • Patients undergoing homeopathy treatment
  • • Families managing multiple prescriptions
  • • Homeopathy clinics and practitioners

Core Problem

Homeopathy treatments often include multiple medicines with specific timings, potencies (30C, 200C, 1M), and food-related instructions. Patients struggle to remember complex schedules, leading to missed doses and reduced treatment effectiveness.

Proposed Solution

A dual-platform system: Doctor Dashboard (web) for creating digital prescriptions and tracking adherence, and Patient Mobile App for receiving smart reminders, visual medicine identification, and dose confirmation. The platform is specifically designed for homeopathy workflows, including potency tracking and food-related reminders.

Expected Impact

  • ✓ Improved adherence: Reduce missed doses by 60-80%
  • ✓ Better outcomes: Patients follow prescriptions correctly, leading to faster recovery
  • ✓ Doctor efficiency: Reduce clarification calls and improve patient management
  • ✓ Digital transformation: Modernize homeopathy practice in India
